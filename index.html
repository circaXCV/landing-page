What is Cinders?

Cinders is a Dark Souls 3 mod that aims to provide a fresh experience to gameplay, with new feature added to further enhance replayability. These featuers include unique game modes, non-linear progression, enemy and bosses with adjusted stats and placements, new bosses, NPCs, classes, weapons, armor sets, and more!
